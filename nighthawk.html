Nighthawk Setup Steps

Step 1: Ask your customer to download and install the Nighthawk App from the App Store for Apple devices or from the Play Store for Android devices.

Troubleshooting:

Turn On the Mobile data and download the Nighthawk App from the App Store for Apple Devices and from Play Store for Android Devices. Once the download is completed install the App and Turn Off Mobile data.

Step 2: Ask your Customer to connect the Mobile device or the Tablet to the Router's Wi-Fi Network Name and Password. Your router’s default WiFi network information is printed on the Router Label on your router. (Tips: Wifi Network Name: Netgear12, Password: Watermelon789. Username will be Netgear with 2 numbers and Password will be like 2 words with 3 numbers.)

Connection Instructions:

1. Turn on Wi-Fi: Make sure Wi-Fi is enabled on your mobile device. You can usually find this in the settings menu.

2. Open Wi-Fi Settings: Go to the Wi-Fi settings on your device. This is typically found under "Settings" > "Wi-Fi" or "Network & Internet."

3. Find Your Network: Look for your Netgear Wi-Fi network in the list of available networks. The network name (SSID) is usually printed on a label on the router.

4. Select the Network: Tap on your Netgear Wi-Fi network name.

5. Enter the Password: If your network is secured, you will need to enter the Wi-Fi password. This password is also typically found on the router label.

6. Connect: After entering the password, tap "Connect." Your device should now connect to the network.

7. Confirm Connection: Once connected, you should see a Wi-Fi icon in the status bar of your device.

Step 3: Yes, my mobile or tablet is connected to the Router's WIFI Network Name.

Step 4: Setup Instructions

Tap on New System Setup and follow the onscreen instructions.

  1. Reboot your Modem - Tap on Next
  2. Connect your Router - Check the ethernet cable from Modem to Router (Tips: From WAN Port to Internet port of your router which will be Yellow colour port) - Tap on Next
  3. Connect to Router's WIFI (WIFI Network Name (SSID) and Network Key (Password) (Tips: Please do not change the WIFI network name and password, leave it as default. Later you can change the SSID name and Password from the APP itself or the device will be disconnected from the default settings and you have to reconnect to the new SSID name and Password.) - Tap on Next
  4. On the Nighthawk App you will see Router Detected
    1. Cables Connected
    2. Internet Connected
    Tap on Next
  5. Next screen will show as Router is now active. Let's personalize your network settings.
  6. Create WIFI Network - Personalize your Wifi network name (SSID) and Password. Leave it as default. Select - Use the same password for Admin Login - Enable this option - Tap on Next
  7. Setup Security questions - To recover your Admin password, set security questions and answers - Tap on Next
  8. Applying Configuration - Wait while we are updating your router settings (Tips: Please do not change the WIFI network name and password, leave it as default. Later you can change the SSID name and Password from the APP itself or the device will be disconnected from the default settings and you have to reconnect to the new SSID name and Password.)
  9. Checking for Firmware Update - Wait about 2 minutes while your router checks for new firmware (Tips: If there is a new firmware version available, please read the release notes before you initiate the firmware update.)
  10. Setup Completed - Congratulations your router is now ready to use. Tap on Finish
